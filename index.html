PROJECT SHOWCASE

Arms of Love: Building a Nonprofit Website
As part of a team, we designed a website from scratch for a local Nonprofit. This design will be implemented for the clients.
Dogger: Microinteractions & Voice Command
A cute micro-interaction paired with voice command input. The idea is to be able to feed your dog while you are away from home. An example of multi-platform modality.

We redesigned the Dept. of Energy’s website to be cleaner, simpler, and easier to use. Check out the process with the button below.
CARGO: A New Way to Rideshare.
CARGO allows for users to rideshare traditionally similar to Uber or Lyft, but now users have an option to have someone transport their personal vehicles to a desired location.
Xplorer: We’ll Plan Your Trip For You.
Xplorer helps travelers eliminate the hassle of choosing flights, accommodations, and activities. Users fill out surveys, advanced web-scraping techniques search for those preferences, and an AI predicts the 5 best options for the user.

About Marcus J. Moraru

Hi there! I am someone who loves learning and teaching myself all kinds of weird stuff. Continuously learning and growing is my passion and my major strength.. I am able to solve most problems that enter my life, and look for help when I cannot do it on my own. Although I have a degree in biology and loved every second of learning about science, I have always been fascinated with technology and design. Tinkering & toying with tech tantalizes my temporal lobes tremendously!

I decided to start my career in UX when I discovered the need for human centered design. As someone who is naturally empathetic, I love finding out why people do things the way they do. Part of my effectiveness is putting my self into other people’s perspectives and playing from there. I aim to bring my own unique perspectives from my science background, and create a synthesis of good design, art, science, and technology.
